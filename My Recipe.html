MY FAVOURITE RECIPES

This is a collection of my favourite recipes.

Beans

This is absolutely my favourite.

List Of Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Salt
  • Maggi
  • Palm oil

Method Of Preparation

  1. Pick the beans.
  2. Put water on fire.
  3. Add the beans to the water.
  4. Add the other ingredidents.
  5. Cooking beans takes time so it doesn't get done quick.

Rice

Everybody loves rice.

List Of Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Onion

Method Of Preparation

  1. Put water on fire.
  2. Wash the rice.
  3. Add the rice to the water.
  4. Add other ingredients.
  5. Rice doesn't take long to cook, gets done after some minutes.

Fried egg

List Of Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Groundnut oil
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Onion

Method Of Preparation

  1. Break the egg to get the slimy yoke.
  2. Add pepper, salt, onions and other ingredients.
  3. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Add it to the groundnut oil on fire.
  5. Done after few monutes.
